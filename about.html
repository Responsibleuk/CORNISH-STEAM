We envision a future where immersive storytelling through transmedia and spatial mediums sparks creativity and exploration. By seamlessly integrating VR video, 3D models, 2D content, and spatial audio with XR technologies, we strive to create unparalleled depth and immersion. Our goal is to intricately weave these themes and mediums into a cohesive narrative that evokes awe and maintains a profound connection between the audience and the location-based experiences in Cornwall.

To realize this vision, we actively explore new collaborations and communication techniques to craft digital and physical STEAM stories. Our aim is to forge pathways that blend VR, AR, mixed reality, and other cutting-edge technologies, inviting our audiences to embark on a captivating journey that encourages exploration and deepens their connection to Cornwall's awe-inspiring environments, places, and culture.

Imagine immersing yourself in a VR experience that delves into Cornwall's mining heritage, while also exploring its diverse fauna and flora, and experiencing the intersection of art and culture. Our journey begins at South Wheal Frances, a catalyst for connecting with new audiences, to engage with and share Cornwall's rich heritage, natural wonders and digital futures. Cornish STEAM is powered by transdisciplinary collaboration and by partnering with Deep Digital Cornwall and showcasing exclusive digital art, we are equipped with the necessary expertise, skills, and high-quality offerings to bring our vision to life.
