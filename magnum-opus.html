Middlemiss Art

In collaboration with Cornish Steam, our vision is to preserve and promote Jon Middlemiss's artistic legacy, uniting his captivating art with the innovation of digital ceramics. We ignite curiosity and foster a deeper understanding of Middlemiss's artistry, serving as a gateway for discovery.

As custodians of his legacy, we curate a collection with Cornish Steam, showcasing three digital ceramics embodying Middlemiss's essence. We inspire both collectors and enthusiasts, bridging traditional art with cutting-edge technology. Through immersive experiences, we connect generations and ensure Middlemiss's brilliance leaves an indelible mark on the artistic landscape. Together, we celebrate and preserve the enduring legacy of this extraordinary artist.

TIMELESS FUSION
Visit Site

Jon Middlemiss (1949-2021) is a celebrated contemporary artist, advocated for environmentalism, ecology, and personal philosophy through his renowned art. Middlemiss's creations embody the concept of "wild intelligence" and connectedness with nature. Influenced by the groundbreaking The Bauhaus, a German art school from 1919 to 1933, blended art, craft, and technology, while the St Ives art movement emerged in the 1930s in Cornwall, where Middlemiss had a significant career presence. With his unique vision, he embraced the interplay between tradition and innovation, seamlessly blending past and present, ancient and modern. His timeless creations bore testament to the harmonious coexistence of artistic mastery and technological prowess. It was through this alchemical synthesis that Middlemiss crafted his mesmerizing artworks.

Middlemiss skillfully incorporates geometric shapes, patterns, and natural elements into his three-dimensional sculptures and installations. Drawing inspiration from mathematics and engineering, his art establishes a deep connection to ecology and landscape, intersecting with STEM fields such as mathematics, engineering, and environmental science.

Middlemiss's work's artistic and creative elements seamlessly merge with the Arts component of STEAM education. Through visually captivating mediums, his art communicates scientific and environmental concepts, providing a platform for exploring and expressing personal theories. Notably, Middlemiss explores the concept of "Mindscapes," highlighting the unique perceptions influenced by cultural backgrounds, experiences, and psychology. This concept fosters understanding and respect for diverse perspectives, benefiting social and cultural contexts.

Middlemiss's philosophy of wild intelligence aligns with sustainability principles crucial for future digital spaces. By learning from nature and adopting a holistic and sustainable approach to technological advancements, wild intelligence offers valuable insights.

The integration of artistic, scientific, and technological concepts in Middlemiss's work resonates with STEAM education themes. His association with the Bauhaus and St Ives movements underscores the significance of progressive art and its integration into everyday life. By promoting environmental awareness and embracing creativity in the 21st Century Industrial Revolution, Middlemiss's art connects with new audiences, bridging disciplines and fostering environmental consciousness in pursuit of a sustainable future..
